GLOBAL EVANGELICAL WOMEN MINISTRY

The Global Evangelical Wonen’s Ministry (GEMEN) caters for the interest of all the men in the Church. The Men’s Ministry organises seminars, workshops, lectures and symposia on all aspects of life including: Marriage enrichment, husbands’ responsibilities at home, child care and education; business management and techniques; leadership and responsibility in the Church and the State; current affairs at both national and international levels; the teaching of basic principles of domestic law and other specialized men-related ministries.
